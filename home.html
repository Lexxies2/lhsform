WHAT IS ESUMBONG?

Lagro High School students can file complaints with this program, ESumbong.If you have witnessed vandalism
at our school or have been the victim of bullying or harassment. ESumbong will assist students in being
more responsible, overcomingtheir fears of school, and developing good behavior.
